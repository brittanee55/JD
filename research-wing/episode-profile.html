Weekly Challenge

Verify One Historical Claim

Choose one claim from the episode. Find one supporting source, one questioning source, and one neutral historical source.

  1. Write the claim in one sentence.
  2. Identify who made the claim.
  3. Find at least two credible sources.
  4. Separate documented facts from interpretation.
  5. Write what remains uncertain.
