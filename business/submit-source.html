Submit a Source

Help improve the Human Knowledge Atlas by submitting books, public records, academic papers, archives, articles, interviews, historical documents, and source corrections.

Editorial Note: Submitted sources do not automatically become part of the Atlas. Every source should go through review, verification, categorization, and editorial assessment.

Source Guidelines

What Makes a Strong Source?

Primary Sources

Official documents, archives, interviews, court records, government records, historical documents.

Secondary Sources

Books, research papers, investigative journalism, scholarly analysis.

Source Context

Explain why the source matters and what Atlas content it connects to.

Corrections

Help improve accuracy by identifying errors, omissions, or missing context.

Source Categories

Atlas Research Standards

Documented

Supported by credible evidence and records.

Claimed

Reported or stated but not fully verified.

Disputed

Credible sources disagree.

Open Question

More research is needed.
