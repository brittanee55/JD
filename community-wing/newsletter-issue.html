Weekly Challenge

Verify One Historical Claim

Pick one claim from the episode. Find one source that supports it, one source that questions it, and one neutral historical source.

  1. Write the claim in one sentence.
  2. Identify who made the claim.
  3. Find two or more sources.
  4. Separate facts from interpretation.
  5. Write what remains uncertain.
